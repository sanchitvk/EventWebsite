YCCE's seminars, aimed at faculty, usually comprise of topics that empower them with not only knowledge and skills but also clarify their thinking required for today’s new-age teachers to possess and practice with their learners. Our seminars are apt for today’s teaching professionals who are looking to brush up their existing skills and can even be attended by parents, consultants and others related to the education sector.

YCCE's workshops designed for teachers and are conducted to have a clear and practical focus to acquaint teachers with the latest teaching methodologies and develop their teaching skills so that they can deliver an effective teaching session for their learners. The workshops are meant to help the participants upgrade themselves as professionals and also learn about the various new career opportunities for professionals working in the education segment.

YCCE’s webinars are especially designed for those who are looking to directly interact with their trainers and peers from the comfort of their homes. With a simulated classroom experience it is aimed at teachers, to equip them with not only the knowledge and skills but also offering them a platform to discuss their ideas and views with their peers and develop the competencies while taking the course from anywhere.
